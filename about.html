About Me

I write about programming and mathematics. However, you'll also find essays on a variety of other topics that catch my interest, since I enjoy exploring different subjects and sharing my thoughts. Inspired by a favorite quote from Naval Ravikant, I embrace this broad approach to my writing:

Specialisation is for insects. I don't believe in this model of trying to focus your life down one thing. You've got one life just do everything you want. - Naval Ravikant
I guess that this to some extent also gives a more authentic view of my personality. To help you navigate, I've labeled these diverse posts as “Rant”s. Feel free to skip them if they aren't your cup of tea. Also, this entire blog is an experiment to improve my writing, giving me accountability by publishing it publicly. I live by Crocker's Rules, so feel free to roast me. (If you want to do so anonymously, you can do that here. Leave a google forms or similar if you want me to respond.) My blog is boring if I never regret a blog post. Also, just feel free to reach out either way. I made this blog to connect with like-minded individuals.
